Domestic Nightmare - Artist Garden GameJam

Domestic Nightmares is a collaboration game that we created during a game GameJam with the 3d animations students.

It is based on the artwork of Louise Bourgeois a french artist. We used her work to inspire our game and tried to convey themes potrayed in her artwork in our game.

The build was made from Unity game engine but the 3d animation students used Unreal Engine to create how the scene would look like. We then used the scene to as a reference to recreate it in unity.

I feel that this project gave me a boosted confidence to work and collaborate with people and being able to handle game management overall.

The rough ideas and references can be found in the manual linked above (the file icon).
